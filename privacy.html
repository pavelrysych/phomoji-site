Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Phomoji ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Pavel Rysych. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Phomoji mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, used for authentication via Supabase
  • Photos and videos — we access your device gallery to display random photos. Emotion tags you assign are stored on our servers linked to your account
  • User ID — a unique identifier assigned to your account for app functionality and analytics
  • Purchase history — collected via RevenueCat to manage your subscription
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, collected via PostHog for product analytics
  • Crash data — error logs collected via Sentry to fix bugs and improve stability

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To analyze how users interact with the app
  • To fix crashes and technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database and authentication
  • RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) — subscription management
  • PostHog (posthog.com) — product analytics
  • Sentry (sentry.io) — crash reporting

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Photo Access

Phomoji accesses your photo library only to display photos within the app. We do not upload your original photos to our servers without your explicit action.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@phomoji.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Phomoji is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
