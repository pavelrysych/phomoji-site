Effective date: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Phomoji ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Pavel Rysych. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Phomoji mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, used for authentication via Supabase
  • Photos and videos — we access your device gallery to display random photos. Emotion tags you assign are stored on our servers linked to your account
  • User ID — a unique identifier assigned to your account for app functionality and analytics
  • Purchase history — collected via RevenueCat to manage your subscription
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, collected via PostHog for product analytics
  • Crash data — error logs collected via Sentry to fix bugs and improve stability

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To fix crashes and technical issues

Your data is stored on Supabase servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
